# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s:

1.Maintain a professional tone in public posts
2.Verify information before sharing
3.Respect others’ opinions and engage politely
4.Use privacy settings to control visibility
5.Showcase achievements and positive activities

## Don’ts:

1.Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
2.Do not share confidential or sensitive information
3.Avoid spreading misinformation or rumors
4.Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
5.Avoid oversharing personal details
