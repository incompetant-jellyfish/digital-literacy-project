# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## Do’s:

Maintain a professional tone in public posts

Verify information before sharing

Respect others’ opinions and engage politely

Use privacy settings to control visibility

Showcase achievements and positive activities

## Don’ts:

Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content

Do not share confidential or sensitive information

Avoid spreading misinformation or rumors

Do not engage in online arguments or trolling

Avoid oversharing personal detail
