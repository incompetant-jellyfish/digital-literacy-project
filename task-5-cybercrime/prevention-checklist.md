# **Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist for College Students in India**

* Always double-check links before clicking, especially those received through email, SMS, or WhatsApp. If it looks urgent or threatening, verify first.

* Never share OTPs, PINs, passwords, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank or a trusted company.

* Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts instead of reusing the same one everywhere.

* Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for important accounts like email, banking, and social media.

* Avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi networks such as in cafes, hostels, or railway stations.

* Download apps only from official sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store, and check reviews before installing.

* Be careful while uploading personal documents (Aadhaar, PAN, college ID) online. Share them only on trusted and verified platforms.

* Regularly check your bank statements and credit report to spot any unusual activity early.

# **UPI / Financial Safety Tips:**

* Never approve a UPI “Collect Request” unless you are expecting to pay someone. Receiving money does not require approval.
* Do not scan random QR codes sent by strangers or posted online. They can lead to money being debited from your account.

# **If something goes wrong, report immediately:**

* National Cyber Crime Portal: [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* Helpline Number: **1930**
